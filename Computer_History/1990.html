Computer History

A timeline that shows the history of computers from 1970's to all the way to present day.

1970's 1980's 1990's 2000's 2010's

Advancements of graphics and music on PC

The Pentium microprocessor advances the use of graphics and music on PC. It was a time where using software for music or to play games become wildly more popular.

PC's becoming gaming machines

Games such as "Command and Conquer," "Alone in the Dark 2," "Theme Park," and "Little Big Adventure" were the one of the first games to use the "new" processors to its full potential.

Google search engine

Sergey Brin and Larry Page develop the Google search engine at Stanford University.

Wi-Fi

The term Wi-Fi becomes part of the computing language and users begin connecting to the internet without wires.

Computer defeats master chess player

Tim Berners-Lee, a researcher at CERN, develops HyperText Markup Language (HTML), giving rise to the World Wide Web.
